Olive Essence: A Journey Through Our Olive Press

An olive press, or olive oil mill, is a facility where olives are pressed to extract their oil. This process has been a fundamental part of Mediterranean culture for centuries. The olives undergo various stages, from cleaning and crushing to pressing and separating the oil from the pulp. The result is the rich, golden olive oil highly valued for its culinary and health benefits. Olive presses play a crucial role in preserving the tradition of producing high-quality olive oil while embracing modern techniques for efficiency

Peat industry

We provide you with wet Olive Cake, which are semi-solid by-products with a strong aroma and a doughy texture known as peat or olive heaviness. It can be used for heating, cooking or making charcoal because it stays burning longer. It can also be used to make animal feed because it is rich in fiber.
